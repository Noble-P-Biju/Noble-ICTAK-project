Welcome To The Home Page Of Task Management System

Add Task: Create and organize your tasks by providing a title, description, and due date. This feature helps you manage your to-do list efficiently.

View Task: Easily access and review your tasks. View essential details such as title, due date, and status at a glance for effective task tracking.

Search Task: Quickly find specific tasks by using the search functionality. This allows you to locate tasks based on keywords or specific criteria.

Filter Task by Category: Organize and categorize your tasks by assigning them to specific categories. Filter tasks based on categories to streamline your view and focus on relevant tasks.

Delete Task: Remove tasks that are no longer needed. This feature helps you declutter your task list and keep it up to date with your current priorities.
